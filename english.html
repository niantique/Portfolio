DESCRIPTION

My name is Nina, I am 29 years old and I’m training as a web and mobile web developer at Human Booster near Lyon. Following my art studies, I was a visual merchandiser for 5 years, an experience that allowed me to develop my creative spirit and my sense of organization. At 28 years old, I felt the need to ask myself: what is my true passion and in which field would I like to dedicate the next few years of my life? It was then that I decided to turn to the world of development.
I am currently looking for an internship from 19/09/2025 to 18/11/2025.
